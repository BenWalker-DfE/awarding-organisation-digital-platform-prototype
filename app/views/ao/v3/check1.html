What is the qualification type?

It is important to consider whether your qualification is primarily academic or technical. 

Academic qualifications are those whose primary purpose is to support students to progress into higher education. These will be approved in a limited range of subjects which are set out in the Qualifications Funding Approval Manual.

Technical qualifications are those whose primary purpose is entry to skilled employment, and which deliver the knowledge, skills and behaviours students need to progress into or within skilled employment, or to higher technical study. If a qualification is aligned to an occupation on the Institute for Apprenticeships and Technical Education's occupational maps, it is generally more likely to be a technical qualification. 

Making this distinction informs whether you should submit qualifications through the academic or technical process. Please be aware, submitting your qualification through the incorrect process risks your submission being invalid.
