Apply for qualification funding

This service is for Ofqual-regulated awarding organisations to apply for funding for both non-technical and technical post-16 qualifications.

Department for Education (DfE), Institute for Apprenticeships and Technical Education (IfATE) and Office of Qualifications and Examinations Regulation (Ofqual) will all have access to the information submitted.

You can expect your application to start being reviewed within 2 weeks from the date of submission.

Start now

Before you start

Check your qualification is in scope of this funding approval process. The Funding Approval Manual lists those qualification types that will automatically be approved for funding. You do not need to continue for us to consider these qualifications for funding approval.

This submission process is only for qualifications with a first teach from 1 August 2026.

You will need a DfE Sign-in account to access Apply for qualification funding.
