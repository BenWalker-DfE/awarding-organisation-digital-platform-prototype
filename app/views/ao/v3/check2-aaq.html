Eligibility

Is your qualification in scope of this funding approval process?

These are the qualification types in scope of this funding approval process:

  • alternative academic qualifications (AAQs) at level 3
  • technical qualifications at level 2 and level 3
  • technical and academic progression qualifications, and T Level foundation qualifications, at level 2

Some qualification types are automatically approved for funding as detailed in the Funding Approval Manual (opens in new tab). You do not need to use this service for us to consider these qualifications for funding approval.
