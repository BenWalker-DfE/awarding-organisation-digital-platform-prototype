What is the reason for less than 100 publicly funded enrolments?

Supporting evidence needs to explain why once the qualification is established it will not have 100 publicly funded enrolments or more. It can include letters from professional bodies or employers that explain why the qualification is specialist. Evidence must include the qualification title (as it is in the submission form) and explain why demand is less than 100.
